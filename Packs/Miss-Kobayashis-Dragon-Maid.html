Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid Custom Minecraft GUI Pack

Immerse yourself in the whimsical world of the Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid Custom Minecraft GUI Pack, featuring textures inspired by the heartwarming anime. This pack brings a playful blue and pink color palette to your Minecraft experience, perfect for fans of both Minecraft and Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid. Enjoy redesigned elements like the hot bar and various containers, all carefully crafted to enhance your gameplay.

Introduction to Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid GUI Pack

This is a GUI-only resource pack inspired by the anime "Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid." It can be used with other resource packs by placing it on top. Various GUI elements have been customized, including the hot bar, with a blue and pink color palette to suit the theme. If you'd prefer other colors like gray and white, feel free to suggest them for future updates. If you find any bugs, please report them, and I will fix them as soon as possible.
