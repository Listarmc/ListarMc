Mushoku Tensei Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft experience with the Mushoku Tensei GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the aesthetic of the anime Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation to your game, offering a unique, customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs.

About the Mushoku Tensei GUI Pack

This GUI-only resource pack seamlessly integrates with your existing resource packs. It updates the hot bar and various container images with themes from Mushoku Tensei. The current color palette includes Orange and Pink, but suggestions for other colors like Yellow or Green are welcome. Your feedback is valuable and can influence future updates!
