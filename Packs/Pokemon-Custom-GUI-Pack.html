Pokémon Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft experience with the Pokémon GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the vibrant aesthetics of the Pokémon series to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs.

About the Pokémon GUI Pack

This GUI-only resource pack is themed around the beloved Pokémon series. It features updates to the hot bar and various UI elements with a Yellow & Blue color palette. Suggestions for other color themes, like Pink & Red, are welcome. Your feedback is valuable and can help shape future updates!
