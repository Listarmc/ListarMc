Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood Minecraft GUI Pack

Elevate your Minecraft gameplay with the Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood Minecraft GUI Pack. This pack brings the unique aesthetic of the popular anime series to your game, offering a customized interface that integrates seamlessly with other resource packs.

About the Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood GUI Pack

The Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack that enhances your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood. Just place this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy the new look for various UI elements, including an updated hotbar. This pack uses a color palette of red, yellow, and orange to match the series' themes, but we are open to creating other color schemes, such as blue and white, based on community interest.

If you encounter any bugs or have feedback, please report them on our Discord server so we can address them promptly.
