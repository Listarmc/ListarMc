Bleach Custom Minecraft GUI Pack

Immerse yourself in the intense world of Bleach with the Bleach Custom Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack features a White and Orange color palette that reflects the essence of the anime, making your Minecraft gameplay visually striking. The GUI elements, including the hot bar and various containers, have been thoughtfully redesigned to enhance your experience.

About the Bleach GUI Pack

The Bleach Custom Minecraft GUI Pack is designed to bring the energy and aesthetics of the "Bleach" anime into your Minecraft world. This GUI-only resource pack can be easily layered over other packs, providing a custom interface that mirrors the show's iconic themes. If you have preferences for other color combinations, such as Red and Black, we encourage you to share your ideas for future updates.

If you encounter any bugs or issues, please report them on our Discord server, and we will address them promptly.
