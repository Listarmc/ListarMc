Kill La Kill Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Kill La Kill Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the intense aesthetics of the anime Kill La Kill to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a unique experience.

About the Kill La Kill GUI Pack

The Kill La Kill GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime Kill La Kill. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features blue and red, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as yellow and blue. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
