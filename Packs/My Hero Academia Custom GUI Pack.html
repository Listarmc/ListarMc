My Hero Academia Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft experience with the My Hero Academia GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the vibrant aesthetics of the anime My Hero Academia to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs.

About the My Hero Academia GUI Pack

This GUI-only resource pack is inspired by the popular anime My Hero Academia, also known as Boku no Hīrō Akademia. It features changes to the hot bar and other UI elements with a Yellow & Blue color palette. Suggestions for other color themes, like Red & White, are welcome. Your feedback is appreciated and can help shape future updates!
