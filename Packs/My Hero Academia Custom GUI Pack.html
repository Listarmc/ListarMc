My Hero Academia Custom GUI Pack

Introduction

This is a GUI-only resource pack themed towards the show "Boku no Hīrō Akademia," also known as "My Hero Academia." This pack includes changes to the hot bar and a color palette of Yellow & Blue. If you have any preferences for other colors, like Red & White, please let me know and I will consider them for future updates. If you encounter any bugs, please report them and I will fix them as soon as possible.
