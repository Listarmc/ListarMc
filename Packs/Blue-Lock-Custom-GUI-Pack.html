Blue Lock Custom Minecraft GUI Pack

Introduction to Blue Lock GUI Pack

The Blue Lock Custom Minecraft GUI Pack is inspired by the intense anime "Blue Lock." This GUI-only resource pack integrates smoothly with other packs, offering a custom interface that captures the show's competitive energy. The pack features a new hot bar and container designs with a color palette of light blue, green, and purple. If you have preferences for other colors, such as red and white, feel free to suggest them for future updates.
