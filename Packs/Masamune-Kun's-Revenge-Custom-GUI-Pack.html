Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Masamune-Kun's Revenge Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the distinctive aesthetics of the anime Masamune-Kun's Revenge to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a unique experience.

About the Masamune-Kun's Revenge GUI Pack

The Masamune-Kun's Revenge GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime Masamune-Kun's Revenge. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features shades of purple and blue, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as yellow and white. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
