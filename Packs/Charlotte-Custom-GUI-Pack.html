Charlotte Custom Minecraft GUI Pack

Introduction to Charlotte GUI Pack

The Charlotte Custom Minecraft GUI Pack infuses your Minecraft experience with the captivating essence of the anime "Charlotte." This GUI-only resource pack can be easily layered on top of your existing texture packs, offering a customized interface with a red and white color theme. We've updated the hot bar and added unique images to various containers. If you encounter any issues or bugs, please report them, and I'll address them promptly.
