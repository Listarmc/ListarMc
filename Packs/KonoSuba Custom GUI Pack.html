KonoSuba Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the KonoSuba Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the whimsical and vibrant aesthetics of the anime KonoSuba to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a unique experience.

About the KonoSuba GUI Pack

The KonoSuba GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime KonoSuba. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features purple and orange, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as pink and white. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
