Toradora! Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft experience with the Toradora! Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the heartwarming and dramatic atmosphere of the anime Toradora! to your game, featuring a custom-designed interface that can be layered over your existing resource packs.

About the Toradora! GUI Pack

The Toradora! GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack inspired by the anime Toradora!. It offers a color palette featuring Dark Blue and Red, with custom images for various UI elements, including the hot bar. Future updates may introduce additional color schemes like White and Black based on community feedback. Your suggestions and bug reports are always welcome!
