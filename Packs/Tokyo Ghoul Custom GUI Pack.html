Tokyo Ghoul Minecraft GUI Pack

Immerse yourself in the dark world of Tokyo Ghoul with the Tokyo Ghoul Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the intense atmosphere of the anime to your Minecraft game, featuring a custom-designed interface that can be layered over your existing resource packs.

About the Tokyo Ghoul GUI Pack

The Tokyo Ghoul GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack inspired by the anime Tokyo Ghoul. It offers a unique color palette of light blue, black, and yellow, with custom images for various UI elements, including the hot bar. Future updates may introduce additional color schemes like red and white based on community feedback. Your suggestions and bug reports are always welcome!
