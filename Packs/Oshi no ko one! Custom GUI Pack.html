Enhance your Minecraft experience with the Oshi no Ko One! GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the vibrant aesthetics of the anime Oshi no Ko One! to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs.

About the Oshi no Ko One! GUI Pack

This GUI-only resource pack is themed around the popular anime Oshi no Ko One!. It features changes to the hot bar and various UI elements with a Purple & Pink color palette. Suggestions for other color themes, like Gray & White, are welcome. Your feedback is valuable and can help shape future updates!
