Oshi no Ko One! Custom GUI Pack

Introduction

This is a GUI-only resource pack themed towards the show "Oshi no Ko One!". Feel free to use it with other resource packs by placing it on top of the pack you use. I've changed the hot bar and experimented with adding different pictures to each container. The color palette I chose is Purple & Pink, but I'm open to suggestions for other colors like Gray & White. If you find any bugs, please report them, and I'll fix them as soon as I can. Comments and feedback are welcome!
