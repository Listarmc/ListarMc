Rent-A-Girlfriend Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft experience with the Rent-A-Girlfriend GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the aesthetic of the anime Rent-A-Girlfriend to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs.

About the Rent-A-Girlfriend GUI Pack

This GUI-only resource pack is themed around the popular anime Rent-A-Girlfriend. It features a customized hot bar, a color palette of Dark Blue & Red, and updated visuals including pink borders and new pictures for containers. Suggestions for other color schemes, like White & Black, are welcome. Updated for version 1.20.1, this pack brings a fresh look to your Minecraft experience. Report any bugs, and they will be promptly fixed.
