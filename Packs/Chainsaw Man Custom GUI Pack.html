Introduction to Chainsaw Man GUI Pack

The Chainsaw Man Custom Minecraft GUI Pack brings the intense and thrilling atmosphere of the Chainsaw Man anime to your Minecraft interface. This GUI-only resource pack can be seamlessly integrated with other packs, allowing you to enjoy a unique interface without compromising your current setup. The pack features a bold color palette of Orange and Blue, enhancing the visual experience. We are open to customizing the colors based on community feedback, so feel free to suggest alternatives like Pink and Red or any other combinations you prefer.
