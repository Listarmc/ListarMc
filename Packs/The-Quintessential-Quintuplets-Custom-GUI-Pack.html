About the GUI Pack

The Quintessential Quintuplets GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime The Quintessential Quintuplets. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features blue and orange, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as pink and white. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
