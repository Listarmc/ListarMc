Horimiya Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Horimiya Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the charm of the anime Horimiya to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a unique experience.

About the Horimiya GUI Pack

The Horimiya GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime Horimiya. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features Pink and Yellow, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as Red and White. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
