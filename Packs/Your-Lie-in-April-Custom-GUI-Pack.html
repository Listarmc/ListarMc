Your Lie in April Custom GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Your Lie in April Custom GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the delicate and emotional aesthetics of the anime "Your Lie in April" to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a personalized experience.

About the Your Lie in April GUI Pack

The Your Lie in April GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime Your Lie in April. The color palette features soft blue and soft pink, reflecting the show's gentle tone. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers.
