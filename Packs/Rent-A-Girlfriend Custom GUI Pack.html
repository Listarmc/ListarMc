Rent-A-Girlfriend Custom GUI Pack

Introduction

This is a GUI-only resource pack themed towards the show "Rent-A-Girlfriend." The pack includes a customized hot bar and has a color palette of Dark Blue & Red. I’ve experimented with adding different pictures to each container. If you prefer other color schemes, like White & Black or others, let me know and I’ll consider them for future updates. If you encounter any bugs, please report them, and I will fix them as soon as possible.

Updated for version 1.20.1 with visual updates including pink borders, new pictures to containers, and coloring the Minecraft logo.
