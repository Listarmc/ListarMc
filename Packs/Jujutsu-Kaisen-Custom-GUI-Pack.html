Jujutsu Kaisen Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Jujutsu Kaisen Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the intense visuals of the anime Jujutsu Kaisen to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a unique experience.

About the Jujutsu Kaisen GUI Pack

The Jujutsu Kaisen GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime Jujutsu Kaisen. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features Red and Black, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as Yellow and White. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
