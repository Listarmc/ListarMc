Shikimori's Not Just a Cutie Custom GUI Pack

Introduction

This is a GUI-only resource pack themed after the show "Shikimori's Not Just a Cutie." You can use it alongside other resource packs by placing it on top of your existing pack. I've customized various UI elements and experimented with different images for each container. The palette features blue, pink, and orange. If you have color suggestions for future updates, such as red and white or other combinations, let me know!

If you find any bugs, please report them to me, and I'll fix them as soon as possible.
