Slime Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Slime Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the vibrant aesthetics of the anime That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a unique experience.

About the Slime GUI Pack

The Slime GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features blue, green, and yellow, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as red and purple. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
