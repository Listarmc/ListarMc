Bocchi the Rock! Custom Minecraft GUI Pack

Introduction to Bocchi the Rock! GUI Pack

The Bocchi the Rock! Custom Minecraft GUI Pack transforms your Minecraft interface with a design inspired by the hit anime "Bocchi the Rock!". This GUI-only resource pack is designed to work seamlessly with other packs, simply place it on top of your existing pack to enjoy a refreshed look. The color scheme features black, pink, and yellow, but other combinations like blue and white can be explored based on community interest.

If you encounter any bugs or issues, please report them on our Discord server, and they will be promptly addressed.
