Charlotte Custom GUI Pack

Introduction

This GUI-only resource pack seamlessly integrates with other resource packs. Place it on top of your existing pack to experience a customized interface inspired by the show "Charlotte." I've updated the hot bar and experimented with adding different pictures to each container. The chosen color palette is Red and White. If you encounter any bugs, please report them to me, and I'll fix them as soon as possible.
