Demon Slayer Minecraft GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Demon Slayer Minecraft GUI Pack. This resource pack features a custom GUI inspired by the popular anime series Demon Slayer, perfect for fans looking to bring the anime's aesthetic into their Minecraft experience.

About the Demon Slayer GUI Pack

The Demon Slayer GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to seamlessly integrate with other Minecraft resource packs. Simply place this pack on top of your existing resource pack to enjoy a customized interface that reflects the vibrant colors and themes of Demon Slayer. We've redesigned key elements like the hotbar, and added unique textures and images to various containers, using a color palette inspired by the anime's iconic Green and Red hues. We are open to suggestions for additional color schemes such as Yellow and Blue, or any other combinations you would like to see. Your feedback is valuable, so please reach out to us on our Discord server to share your ideas and report any bugs you encounter.
