Wonder Egg Priority Custom GUI Pack

Enhance your Minecraft gameplay with the Wonder Egg Priority Custom GUI Pack. This resource pack brings the unique aesthetics of the anime "Wonder Egg Priority" to your game, offering a customized interface that can be layered over other resource packs for a personalized experience.

About the Wonder Egg Priority GUI Pack

The Wonder Egg Priority GUI Pack is a GUI-only resource pack designed to transform your Minecraft interface with designs inspired by the anime Wonder Egg Priority. You can easily use this pack on top of your existing resource packs to enjoy updated UI elements, including a revamped hotbar and unique textures for various containers. The current color palette features Blue and Yellow, but we are open to suggestions for other colors such as Pink and Red. Your feedback is always welcome!

If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions, please report them on our Discord server, and we'll address them as quickly as possible.
